Plant-Based Protein Sources

As a plant-based college athlete who loves to lift, I know how important protein consumption can be for muscle growth. Being vegan, the first thing people ask me is, "Where do you get your protein?" A year ago, I'd tell you from my avocado toast I would have everyday at lunch. Now, I can confidently say that I consume 130-145g of protein daily! I am more knowledgeable regarding plant-based proteins and want to help others achieve their daily protein intake!

Vegan Protein Sources:

  • Lentils
  • Beans
  • Nuts
  • Edamame
  • Soymilk
  • Tofu*
  • Tempeh*
  • Seitan*
  • Chia Seed
  • Flax Seed
  • Pumpkin Seed
  • Hemp Seed
  • Edamame Pasta
  • Chickpea Pasta
  • Red or Green Lentil Pasta
  • Protein Powder (pea, rice, etc)
  • Nutritional Yeast (NOOCH)*
  • Whole Wheat Bread
  • Protein Bars
  • PB2 Fit Powder*
  • Texturized Vegetable Protein (TVP)*
  • Vital Wheat Gluten Powder (for baking)

*My personal everyday protein sources
